About Ash Fit

Ashling Smith is the current W45 masters outdoor 1500m and mile national record holder. As well as numerous national titles, she is also silver and bronze European masters team cross country medallist. Ashling's passion for running led her to become closely involved in mentoring club mates and athletes from other disciplines. Observing a frequent cycle of training, competition, injury and rehab prompted her to explore a more preventative approach.


Since 2019 she has qualified as a Sports and Physical Therapist holding ITEC Level 4 Sports Massage, Personal Training, Group Training, QQI Level 6 Psychology for Sports performance and Nutrition for Sports performance among other qualifications.


"I am completely invested in supporting my clients on their journey to fulfilling their potential. I offer a tailored and individual approach for each client which allows them to achieve their health and fitness goals in a safe and sustainable manner. The skills a client acquires with me are skills they can transfer to all aspects of life, in a business or home/personal setting. I like to treat the whole person using an approach which fosters a sense of well being as well as physical stimulation. The client is guided through the various steps of physical fitness and psychological skills for performance. The client is also educated about the importance of nutrient timing to support performance and recovery in training and of course, competition.
