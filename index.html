About Ash Fit

Ashling Smith is the current W45 masters outdoor 1500m and mile national record holder. As well as numerous national titles, she is also silver and bronze European masters team cross country medallist. Since 2019 she has qualified as a Sports and Physical Therapist holding ITEC Level 4 Sports Massage, Personal Training, Group Training, QQI Level 6 Psychology for Sports performance and Nutrition for Sports performance


I am completely invested in supporting my clients on their journey to fulfilling their potential. I offer a tailored and individual approach for each client which allows them to achieve their health and fitness goals in a safe and sustainable manner.
